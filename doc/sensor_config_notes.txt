

Sonic/IRGA
==========

- Must connect to the control unit with ECMon or DevConfig software and 
    * enable unprompted RS-485 output (factory default: disabled)
    * check baud rate (factory default: 115200)
    * check output rate (factory default: 10 Hz)


RS-232/RS-485 converter
=======================

- Dip switches must be changed from 2-wire to 4-wire configuration
- Figure out what "ECHO" dip switches do
- Do bench tests to determine whether bias/termination options are needed
